How to Play

      
    


    

      

        
Round Start

        
Each round starts from the required double. In a standard match that means 12|12 in round 1, then 11|11, and so on down to 0|0.

        

          
          
          
        

      


      

        
Your Turn

        
    
          
  • Select a tile in Your Hand.
    • 
          
  • Tap a playable train to place it.
    • 
          
  • If you cannot play, tap Draw.
    • 
          
  • If you still cannot play, tap Pass.
    • 
        

      


      

        
Open Trains

        
An open train is marked with a green dot 🟢. When a player cannot play after drawing, that train opens and other players may use it.

      


      

        
Doubles

        
If a double is played, it must be satisfied before anything else can happen. Everyone must try to match that open end until it is cleared.

      


      

        
Scoring

        
At the end of a round, all remaining pips in your hand are added to your score. Lowest total score wins the match.
